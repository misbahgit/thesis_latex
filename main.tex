\lhead{\emph{Abstract}}

\abstract{\addtocontents{toc}{\vspace{1em}} % Add a gap in the Contents, for aesthetics

Authentication has been an important issue since the beginning of human civilization, and people use some mechanisms to check the authenticity of a person before divulging any secret or valuable information. When a person was known, biological features such as the face, sound, etc., were good enough to authenticate the person. But when the person was not known, and no one at the receiving end had seen him or heard him, it was a difficult task to authenticate the person. In such cases, people used to carry some artifact, e.g., some personal belongings of the sender, which the receiver could identify to authenticate himself. So, authentication is not a modern-day problem rather, It is an ancient one, though the methods used to authenticate kept changing over time. Nowadays, we are using different mechanisms for this purpose, including login-password, graphical password, token-based authentication, biometric authentication, etc. In this paper, a novel idea has been proposed that can keep the bystanders/ onlookers/ intruders at bay and can keep the password safe.
}
